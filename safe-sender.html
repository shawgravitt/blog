To ensure that you will always receive my emails, it’s highly recommended that you add my email address to your address book/safe list.

This way, my emails will never end up in your spam folder.

HOW TO ADD MY EMAIL ADDRESS TO YOUR SAFE SENDERS LIST OR ADDRESS BOOK?

Look under the email service provider you use to see how.

Gmail

  1. Click the Show search options downward-pointing triangle in your Gmail’s main search field
  2. Type smgravitt@gmail.com in the From field
  3. Click the link “Create filter with this search”
  4. Check the option “Never send it to Spam”
  5. Click Create filter

Mac Mail

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Right-click the sender’s email address
  3. Click Add to contacts in the short-cut menu
  4. Click Save and Close


Microsoft Outlook Express 6+

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Left-click the sender icon, or right click the sender’s name
  3. Click Add to contact
  4. Click Save and close

Microsoft Outlook 2003

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Select Actions on the toolbar
  3. Select Junk Email from the drop-down menu
  4. Select Add sender to Safe Senders List
  5. Verify that my contact information is correct and click Ok

Microsoft Outlook 2007

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Click Options on the Tools menu
  3. On the Preferences tab, under Email, click Junk E-mail
  4. Select Safe Senders or Safe Recipients tab and click add
  5. Enter the email address of the contact and click Ok

AOL 8.0+

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Click Add Address icon
  3. Verify the sender’s contact information

AOL Webmail

  1. Click on the Addresses tab in the upper right corner of the Mailbox window
  2. Click on the New drop-down menu and select New Contact.
  3. Type the email address of the new contact in the Screen Name field and click the Save button.

Earthlink

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Click Add Sender
  3. Verify that my contact information is correct
  4. Click “Yes”

Entourage

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Right-click the sender’s email address
  3. Select Add to Address Book in the short-cut menu
  4. Verify the sender’s contact details

Juno

  1. From the mail screen, click on the Address Book tab
  2. Insert the email address you would like to add
  3. Click Quick Add

Mozilla Thunderbird

  1. Click the Address book button
  2. Make sure the Personal Address Book is highlighted
  3. Click the New Card button
  4. Under the Contact tab, copy and paste my address and click ok

MSN Hotmail (Classic)

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Click Save Address in the menu bar
  3. Verify that my contact information is correct
  4. Click OK on the next screen

Window Live Hotmail
Adding to the Address Book doesn’t automatically add you to the Safe Sender list; you must mark the sender as Safe.

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Click on the Mark as Safe link

Yahoo! Mail

  1. Open an email from me
  2. Select the Add to Address Book link.
  3. Enter the email address of the contact and click on Save Contact.
